02 — The Founder

Pioneer Vision.
Global Reach.

Talita Haggist arrived on Hilton Head Island with a global perspective built over years of experience navigating high-stakes real estate markets on multiple continents. She understood that the buyers drawn to Hilton Head weren't just looking for a house. They were making a life decision.

Raised across cultures and shaped by international experience, Talita built her practice on the belief that real estate is ultimately a deeply human experience. Every transaction carries a family's hopes, a couple's future, or a legacy being built.

"My clients don't just want a beautiful home — they want confidence that their advisor is fully committed to them, from the first call to the closing table."

Today, Talita is the driving force behind BHHP — a pioneer vision backed by a global marketing machine, and an intimate knowledge of the island's most sought-after addresses.
