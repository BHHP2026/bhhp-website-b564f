The Founder

Meet Talita Haggist

Talita Haggist is the founder and principal broker of Best Hilton Head Properties. With more than 20 years of experience on Hilton Head Island, she has built a reputation as one of the most trusted luxury real estate professionals in the Lowcountry.

Born with dual citizenship and raised across cultures, Talita built her practice on the belief that real estate is ultimately a deeply human experience. Every transaction represents a life chapter — and she treats it that way.

“My clients don’t just want a beautiful home — they want confidence that someone is working harder for them than anyone else would.”

Today, Talita leads one of Hilton Head Island’s most trusted luxury teams — bringing white-glove service, global marketing reach, and an intimate knowledge of the island to every client relationship.
