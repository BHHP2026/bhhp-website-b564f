Where Hilton Head’s Most
Coveted Addresses Live

Sea Pines Resort is more than a neighborhood — it’s a self-contained world. Established in 1956 as one of America’s first master-planned communities, it set the standard for resort living that others have spent decades trying to replicate.

Home to the legendary Harbour Town Golf Links — site of the annual RBC Heritage PGA Tour event — and the iconic candy-striped lighthouse, Sea Pines commands the highest property values on the island and consistently attracts discerning buyers from around the world.

The community spans from the Atlantic Ocean to Calibogue Sound, offering oceanfront estates, lagoon-side villas, golf course homes, and everything between. Properties range from lock-and-leave vacation condominiums to sprawling primary residences.

With 24/7 gated security, 15 miles of leisure trails, private beach access, three championship golf courses, and a world-class tennis complex, Sea Pines offers a lifestyle that buyers simply cannot find elsewhere on the island.

Harbour Town

The iconic marina village at the heart of Sea Pines — yacht basin, boutiques, restaurants, and the famous Harbour Town Lighthouse overlooking Calibogue Sound.

Championship Golf

Three award-winning courses including Harbour Town Golf Links, home of the PGA Tour’s RBC Heritage tournament each April.

South Beach

A quieter beach village at the island’s southern tip, with casual dining, kayak rentals, and direct access to unspoiled Atlantic shoreline.

Private & Gated

24-hour security and controlled access at all entry points provides peace of mind for full-time residents and vacation homeowners alike.

15 Miles of Trails

An extensive network of paved leisure paths winds through the community — ideal for cycling, jogging, and exploring the natural beauty of the Lowcountry.

Nature Preserves

Protected forests, lagoons, and wildlife habitats woven throughout the community, ensuring Sea Pines retains its natural character in perpetuity.
