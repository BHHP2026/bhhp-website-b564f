Where Championship
Golf Meets Private Island Life

Sea Pines Resort is more than a neighborhood — it is the defining address on Hilton Head Island. Spanning 5,000 private acres at the island's southern tip, Sea Pines is home to four world-renowned golf courses, including Harbour Town Golf Links, host of the PGA TOUR's RBC Heritage presented by Boeing. Its 5 miles of pristine private beach, 15-mile leisure trail network, and a 605-acre Forest Preserve make it a community unlike any other on the East Coast.

Harbour Town:
The Heart of Sea Pines

The red-and-white striped Harbour Town Lighthouse is the most recognizable landmark on Hilton Head Island. Rising 90 feet above Calibogue Sound, it has guided mariners and golfers alike since 1970. Climb its 114 steps for panoramic views, then step out into one of the East Coast's most charming marina villages.

  • 90-foot lighthouse with 114-step observation deck and panoramic views of Calibogue Sound
  • Deep-water marina with berths for vessels to 100+ feet
  • Quarterdeck Restaurant with rooftop oyster bar and sunset cocktails
  • Curated boutiques, galleries, and waterfront dining
  • Live music and seasonal outdoor events throughout the year
  • Home of the RBC Heritage clubhouse and the famous 18th hole gallery
