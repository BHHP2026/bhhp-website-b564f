Island Living at
Its Most Accessible

Hilton Head Plantation is the largest gated community on Hilton Head Island, covering nearly 4,000 acres along the island’s north end. With more than 4,000 homesites, it offers an extraordinary range of property types — from modest cottages to sweeping Intracoastal waterfront estates — making it one of the island’s most sought-after communities for both primary residents and second-home buyers.

Established in the 1970s, Hilton Head Plantation has matured into a beautifully landscaped, fully amenitized community with a deep sense of neighborhood identity. Unlike many resort communities, it attracts a strong year-round residential population, giving it an authenticity that pure vacation destinations often lack.

The community’s Skull Creek waterfront is one of its crown jewels — a deepwater marina with stunning views across the Intracoastal Waterway toward Pinckney Island and the mainland beyond. Waterfront homes here command significant premiums and rarely stay on the market long.

Four golf courses — Country Club of Hilton Head, Bear Creek, Oyster Reef, and Dolphin Head — offer variety for every level of golfer. Combined with tennis, pickleball, pools, and miles of leisure trails, Hilton Head Plantation delivers an exceptional amenity package at a price point well below Sea Pines or Wexford.

Skull Creek Marina

A deepwater marina on the Intracoastal Waterway with stunning views toward Pinckney Island — boating access that rivals any community on the island.

Four Golf Courses

Country Club of Hilton Head, Bear Creek, Oyster Reef, and Dolphin Head offer 72 holes of championship golf within the community’s gates.

Intracoastal Waterfront

Miles of Intracoastal Waterway frontage with deep-water homesites and dock potential — among the most coveted addresses in the community.

Plantation House

The community’s social hub, offering pools, fitness center, tennis, pickleball, and a full calendar of events for residents year-round.

Diverse Property Types

From affordable villas and patio homes to grand waterfront estates — one of the widest ranges of property types of any community on the island.

Year-Round Community

A strong permanent residential population gives Hilton Head Plantation an authentic neighborhood feel distinct from purely vacation-oriented communities.
