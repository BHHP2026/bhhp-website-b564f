Where Luxury Meets the Lowcountry

Hilton Head's Most Private Waterfront Community

Nestled on Hilton Head Island in the heart of South Carolina's Lowcountry is the exclusive private waterfront community known as Wexford. Boasting a remarkable 37-acre inland harbour with an innovative lock system — one of only three on the entire East Coast — Wexford's facilities and amenities rival only the most high-end private yacht and country clubs in the world. With constant seven-foot water depth and access for vessels to 70 feet, this is a seafarer's dream made real.

The 37-Acre Private Harbour — one of three lock systems on the East Coast
The Crown Jewel of Wexford

A Harbour Like Nowhere Else

At the heart of Wexford is a feat of engineering that defines the community: a 37-acre inland harbour connected to the Intracoastal Waterway via an innovative lock system — one of only three such systems on the entire East Coast. Vessels up to 70 feet can berth here in constant 7-foot water depth, with 24/7 harbormaster service and direct access to Broad Creek and the open ocean.

  • 37-acre freshwater harbour maintained at constant 7-foot depth year-round
  • Lock system access to Intracoastal Waterway and Broad Creek beyond
  • Berths for vessels up to 70 feet with 24/7 harbormaster on duty
  • One of only three inland lock harbour systems on the entire East Coast
  • Deep-water homesites with private docks steps from your back door
  • Immediate proximity to open-water fishing, sailing, and coastal cruising
