Hilton Head’s Most
Exclusive Address

Wexford Plantation is in a class of its own. This ultra-private, gated community on the island’s mid-section is home to some of the most significant estates on Hilton Head — many with deep-water access, private docks, and sweeping views across the Intracoastal Waterway.

With fewer than 500 homesites, Wexford maintains an intimacy and exclusivity that larger communities simply cannot match. Properties change hands rarely, and when they do, they generate intense interest from qualified buyers across the country and internationally.

The community revolves around its private harbour — a full-service marina accommodating vessels up to 60 feet, with direct access to the Intracoastal Waterway and the Atlantic Ocean beyond. For serious boaters, there is no finer address on the island.

Wexford’s Williamsburg-style clubhouse serves as the social heart of the community, hosting the full calendar of events that its tight-knit membership has come to cherish. Tennis, croquet, and the 18-hole Wexford Golf Course complete the amenity picture.

Private Harbour

A full-service deep-water marina with slips for vessels up to 60 feet, direct Intracoastal access, and fuel dock — a boater’s paradise.

Wexford Golf Course

An 18-hole championship course winding through the community’s marshes and lagoons, exclusively for members and their guests.

Deep-Water Homesites

Coveted deep-water lots with private dock potential — the most sought-after real estate category on all of Hilton Head Island.

Williamsburg Clubhouse

The stunning colonial-style clubhouse is the social hub of Wexford life — fine dining, events, and a genuine sense of community among its members.

Tennis & Croquet

Multiple Har-Tru tennis courts and a regulation croquet lawn round out an amenity package that is unmatched in the Lowcountry.

Maximum Privacy

Fewer than 500 homesites, 24-hour roving security, and a single controlled entry point ensure an uncompromising level of privacy for every resident.
