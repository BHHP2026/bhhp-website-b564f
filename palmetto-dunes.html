Where Ocean Meets Championship Living

Hilton Head's Most Celebrated Oceanfront Community

Stretching across 2,000 lush acres along Hilton Head Island's most coveted Atlantic coastline, Palmetto Dunes Oceanfront Resort is a masterwork of resort living. Three miles of pristine private beach frame a community designed for those who demand the extraordinary — from three world-class championship golf courses to an 11-mile interconnected lagoon system that winds through the heart of the resort, offering unmatched water frontage and natural beauty at every turn.

The Palmetto Dunes Difference

An 11-Mile Lagoon Like No Other

The resort's iconic 11-mile continuous lagoon system is arguably Hilton Head's most extraordinary natural feature — a living, tidal waterway that winds through every neighborhood, creating one of the highest concentrations of waterfront homesites on the entire island.

  • Over 11 continuous miles of interconnected tidal lagoon throughout the resort
  • Abundant water-view homesites offering lagoon frontage at all price points
  • World-class bass fishing, kayaking, and paddleboarding directly from your backyard
  • Rich Lowcountry ecosystem — herons, egrets, otters, and native wildlife
  • Three championship courses designed in harmony with the lagoon landscape
  • Toptracer Golf Range: cutting-edge technology for practice and entertainment
  • Shelter Cove Harbour: full-service marina with dining, retail, and live events
