Where Resort Living
Meets Island Authenticity

Palmetto Dunes is one of Hilton Head’s most popular and well-rounded resort communities. Spanning 2,000 acres along the Atlantic Ocean, it offers an exceptional variety of property types — from oceanfront villas and golf course homes to lagoon-side condominiums perfect for vacation rentals.

What sets Palmetto Dunes apart is its remarkable 11-mile lagoon system, which winds through the entire community, offering kayaking, paddleboarding, and fishing right from many backyards. It’s the kind of amenity that turns a vacation property into a lifestyle.

Three championship golf courses — Robert Trent Jones, George Fazio, and Arthur Hills — provide variety and challenge for golfers of every level, while the Palmetto Dunes Tennis & Pickleball Center ranks among the top facilities on the East Coast.

The community’s Shelter Cove Harbour adds a marina village dimension with boutique shops, waterfront dining, and regular evening concerts during the season. Palmetto Dunes consistently delivers an exceptional blend of amenities at a more accessible price point than Sea Pines or Wexford.

Three Golf Courses

Robert Trent Jones, George Fazio, and Arthur Hills courses offer 54 holes of championship golf through some of the island’s most scenic landscapes.

11-Mile Lagoon System

A continuous 11-mile waterway winds through the community — ideal for kayaking, paddleboarding, and catch-and-release fishing.

Oceanfront Access

Miles of protected Atlantic beachfront accessible to all residents, with beach equipment rentals and seasonal lifeguard services.

Tennis & Pickleball

The Palmetto Dunes Tennis & Pickleball Center ranks among the finest facilities on the East Coast, with 23 courts and a full pro staff.

Shelter Cove Harbour

A charming marina village with waterfront dining, boutique shopping, and the popular Tuesday night outdoor concert series.

Vacation Rental Ready

Many Palmetto Dunes properties are approved for short-term rental, making them ideal investment properties with strong seasonal income potential.
